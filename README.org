* Literate SecOps
** Preamble
I was demonstrating how I use Emacs and Org-mode to organize my work. While doing so I found my workflow is unique and reproducible. I had spoken about this idea and it my workflow mimics that already studied. Literate DevOps is a concept developed by Emacs user Howard Abrams. I propose something new for those in the Cybersecurity field: Literate SecOps.
** About
Literate SecOps is the act of defining your documentation, communications, and code.
